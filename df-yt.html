YouTube without distractions

Lately I am trying to ditch websites/apps that are primarily built to keep the user on the website/app for as long as possible. YouTube does this by having endless recommendations for videos similar to the one that you have just watched. Unless you turn the feature off, I believe YouTube even plays these recommended videos for you automatically!

Here I give instructions for setting up newsboat (an RSS feed reader) and mpv (a video player) so that you can watch videos from select YouTube channels without ever having to open the YouTube website (or even a website).

Requirements: Some linux-based operating system
