Kvass

  • 1000ml water
  • 47g toasted rye bread
  • 85g sugar
  • 1/5th tbsp dried instant yeast
  • 10 raisins
Bring water to boil. Remove from heat. Put in the bread and the raisins. Cover with lid and leave overnight to cool to room temperature. Remove bread and raisins, and then filter (e.g. through a coffee filter). Add sugar and dissolve. Add yeast. Cover and leave for around 6 hours, or overnight. Bottle, and leave the bottles at room temperature overnight to build carbonation. Then refridgerate.
