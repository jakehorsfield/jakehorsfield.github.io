J'apprends le français depuis mai 2019. J'utilise:

Books

  • French in Three Months
  • The Oxford French Cartoon-strip Vocabulary Builder
  • Harry Potter à l'école des sorciers

Podcasts

  • Coffee Break French
    I find this a great podcast for learning phrases for everyday situations, but not so great for learning lots of vocabulary fast.
    So I would recommend supplementing with a vocab book, or a textbook that includes vocabulary lists.

Software

  • Anki
    Anki is a piece of software that uses the idea of spaced repetition with flashcards.

TV

  • Some french TV shows: Au service de la France, La Mante, La Trêve, Le Chalet, Le Bureau des légendes
